Interesting Facts about the Taj Mahal

Fact No. Category Details
1 Builder Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal
2 Construction Period Construction started in 1632 and was completed in 1653
3 Material Used Made of white Makrana marble brought from Rajasthan
4 Architecture Perfectly symmetrical structure with Islamic, Persian, and Indian styles
5 Color Effect The marble changes color with sunlight—pink, white, and golden shades
6 Minarets The four minarets lean slightly outward to protect the main tomb
7 Workforce About 20,000 artisans and craftsmen worked on the monument
8 UNESCO Status Declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1983
9 Symbolism Recognized worldwide as a symbol of love
10 Location Situated on the banks of the Yamuna River in Agra, India
